These are preferences that you previously selected in UCAS NetUpdate. They relate to:

  • your contact details, for use by UCAS
  • what UCAS sends to successful applicants
  • what happens when you receive an application

You can no longer change these settings in NetUpdate, as NetUpdate has now been turned off. These settings are included here so that UCAS Apply can continue to work as normal.

What does ‘information unknown’ mean?

Some of the fields below are labelled ‘Information unknown’. This is because UCAS doesn’t have permission under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) to share these details with us.

You can get hold of these details by contacting the UCAS Higher Education Provider Team (HEP Team) on 0344 984 1111 or at hep_team@ucas.ac.uk.

Alternatively you can choose new contacts and we will send them to UCAS to update.
